Pedagogical Actitvities
The learning journey for each villager at TDV is unique, bespoke and everlasting. This journey is supported by three inter-related streams of activities- Villaging, Baskets and Taskbox.
Villaging
Villaging is an idea of being, a state of mind. This (or the orange pipe outside our campus) is where the walls of the village transcend, the inside meets the outside and the villager delves into learning about the self by learning about the larger community around them.
Baskets
For a villager not only dreams the dream but has the ability to realize them, each basket is like a portal to specific experiences and learnings about a facet of the world not yet fully known to them.
Taskbox
These are industry-centric, real-time design projects that the villagers work on, often in groups to certain briefs set by real clients. Each Taskbox is a stand that one takes while juggling between the ideal and the real much like a game of Pitthu, one has to accurately hit the pile of stones but be sure that their team is able to build the pile back up before getting hit.
