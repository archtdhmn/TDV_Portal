Into the Portals
Baskets Overview
As a Villager goes on about their learning journey, they are constantly triggered to develop new ideas and skills through Baskets course. Each of these courses is like a portal that they walk into in order to gather new experiences and perspectives of a world not yet fully known to them.
Metabaskets

Each basket at the village is a cluster of courses grouped together based on a common theme. The theme of the clusters remains constant while the courses can keep on changing or evolving and new ones added. Currently, there are 8 baskets in total which are further grouped together in 3 METABASKETS.
